1. THE FROZEN H1 IS FUNCTIONALLY GRAPH-INDEPENDENT. Deleting the ENTIRE graph neighbourhood — 16,000 to
   60,000 edges — changes its prediction by 1.8e-07 to 4.6e-05 of magnitude, on all 8 distinct val genes

   [h_graph ‖ h_do], so it can be driven entirely by its h_do half at any lambda — lambda measures use of
   the joint pathway, not of the graph.) See docs/feat008-stage-b-notes.md, "The decisive measurement".
2. THE FROZEN H1 IS A ONE-EPOCH MODEL. Its per-epoch val curve (seed 0):
     val.response 3.237, 3.236 (epoch 1 = BEST), 3.239, 3.252, 3.276, 3.273, 3.298, 3.279, 3.316, 3.345,

     model that had trained for essentially one epoch. The graph penalty collapses to ~0 by epoch 3, so
     this is plain overfitting on the response term, not a regulariser fighting the fit.
