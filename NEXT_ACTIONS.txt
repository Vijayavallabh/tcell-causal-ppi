================================================================================================
E1 — MINIMUM-DETECTABLE-EFFECT / POWER ANALYSIS OF THE n=5 NULL      [analysis only, no GPU, ~1h]
================================================================================================
WHAT : From the 5 paired per-seed differences already in robustness_5seed.json, compute the effect size
       this design had 80% power to detect (two-sided, alpha and Bonferroni-across-4 matching the paper),
       and report it beside the observed -0.0009. State the observed |Delta| as a fraction of the MDE.
WHY  : A null is only publishable if it is a *bounded* null. This converts "no significant difference"
       into "any graph benefit larger than +/-X programs/gene would have been detected; we observed
       one 10-20x smaller." It is the cheapest, highest-credibility addition and gates E4's interpretation.
HOW  : One script over robustness_5seed.json: paired-t MDE = (t_{crit}+t_{power}) * sd(diffs)/sqrt(5),
       per contrast. No retrain. Emit a small table + one CI-vs-MDE figure. Cite in the abstract.
GUARD: If the MDE is LARGER than a benefit anyone would care about, the honest conclusion is
       "inconclusive, not null" — say that; do not upgrade parity to a claim the power can't support.

E2 — lambda_graph SWEEP: TURN THE CONFOUND INTO A FIGURE            [runnable now, ~10 lanes, 1 A100]
================================================================================================
WHAT : Screen condition_gated at lambda_graph in {1e-4, 1e-3} x 5 seeds (we already hold 0 and 1e-2):
       plot final gate_mean and the measured h1_vs_no_graph contrast vs lambda. Show the gate-collapse
       threshold and that crossing it silently manufactures a false reading.
WHY  : This is the paper's GENERALIZABLE METHODS LESSON — an unnormalised structural penalty can switch
       off the very mechanism a study means to test, and the ablation still "runs" and reports a number.
       A monotone gate-death curve makes that a figure other graph-ML authors will cite, lifting the
       paper from "one negative result" to "a measurement pitfall + its fix."
HOW  : run_screening.py --only condition_gated --lambda-graph 1e-4 --seed {0..4}  (then 1e-3), separate
       SCREENING_ROOT; then multiseed.py aggregates. Fan the 10 lanes across A100 machines via
       portable_artifacts.txt. gate_mean is already logged per epoch (trainer.py).
GUARD: Pre-register that the sweep is a diagnostic, not a tuning search — you are NOT selecting the best
       lambda for a headline. lambda=0 stays the reported operating point; the sweep only maps the failure.

E3 — EDGE-SOURCE DECOMPOSITION: WHY NO SUBSET OF THE GRAPH HELPS    [runnable now, ~5-15 lanes, 1 A100]
================================================================================================
WHAT : Re-screen condition_gated with the 86%-of-edges functional_assoc tier progressively removed
       (functional_min_score in {0.0, 0.4, 0.9, >1.0=drop-all-functional}), keeping physical + complex.
       Does dropping the noisy functional edges move h1_vs_no_graph off parity?
WHY  : Closes "your graph is 86% low-confidence STRING functional links — of course it's noise." The
       screening-level confirmation of feat-012's inference-time source ablation. If high-confidence /
       physical-only STILL gives parity, the null is a property of the *task-graph relationship*, not of
       edge quality — a much stronger statement. If a subset DOES win, that is a real lead (see guard).
HOW  : graph_builder already has functional_min_score (filters ONLY functional edges; node identity is
       stable across thresholds by construction). Wire it through run_screening as a --functional-min-score
       lane knob (a few lines) or via a small config sweep. NOTE: confidence *pruning as an improvement*
       was refuted by the arch search — frame E3 strictly as a MECHANISM ablation explaining the null,
       not as a tuning knob. A full physical-only / functional-only / complex-only 3-way split needs a
       tiny per-source mask (~5 lines in graph_builder) if a reviewer wants the complete decomposition.
GUARD: Any subset that beats no-graph and SURVIVES both corrections at 5 seeds is a NEW CANDIDATE and
       owes the full protocol (same frozen fold, paired seeds, Bonferroni AND Holm) before any claim.
       promoted.json stays frozen until then.
